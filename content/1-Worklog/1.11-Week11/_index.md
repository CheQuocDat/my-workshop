### Week 11 Achievements:

* **Completed Advanced Frontend Features:**
  * **Mock Interview Workspace:** Built a modern minimalistic interface. Users can listen to the AI interviewer voice, click to start recording their answers, visualize real-time recording progress, and submit assignments.
  * **Interactive Dashboard:** Integrated charts libraries to display correct answer rates by skill category (Frontend, Backend, Cloud) and learning history, helping students target weak spots.

  * Performance: Ensured smooth UI transitions during loading, success, and error states across desktop and mobile devices.
